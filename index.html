Warm Welcome

Welcome to the Laboratory of Microfluidics and Advanced Systems under the direction of Dr. Ye TIAN. We are a multidisciplinary research group of enthusiastic people with backgrounds from chemistry, physics, materials, engineering, medicine and bio-related fields. Our research interests include but not limited to microfluidics, POCT/IVD, soft materials, flexible electronics/wearable devices, biomaterials, tissue engineering, soft robotics, nanomaterials, bionics, 3D printing, sensing/probe, functional surface and intelligent hardware. We are seeking to explore a wide range of state-of-the-art technologies for human healthcare and engineering applications. We enjoy new challenges and collaborations. Feel free to visit us please, either in person or on the web.

​ We welcome highly motivated people who are interested in interdisciplinary research to join our group.
